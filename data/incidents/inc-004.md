# Incident inc-004 — Harbor Terminal container instability

**Reported by:** Harbor Terminal operations at 07:11
**Status:** monitoring
**Casualty estimate:** none

A six-high stack of standard intermodal containers in the southwest
yard shifted approximately 40cm during the quake. No containers fell
and no breach has been observed. Manifests indicate the affected stack
holds dry consumer goods (no hazardous classification).

Terminal operations have suspended yard movements within 50m of the
affected stack. A commercial structural inspector has been engaged.

Recommended actions for command:
1. No life-safety dispatch needed at this time
2. Update once commercial inspector returns assessment (expected ~3 hours)
