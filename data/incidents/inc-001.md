# Incident inc-001 — Riverside Tower partial collapse

**Reported by:** Engine 14 forward observer at 06:42
**Status:** active
**Casualty estimate:** 30+ residents potentially trapped between floors 6–9

The east face of the tower has lost its outer cladding and exterior
balcony assemblies on floors 6 through 9. Two parallel cracks run
diagonally across the south stairwell. Aftershocks are continuing.

Initial responders report:
- Fire on floor 4 (electrical, contained to one apartment)
- Multiple voice contacts from inside floors 7 and 8
- North stairwell appears intact and structurally sound
- South stairwell is unsafe for evacuation

Recommended actions for command:
1. Confirm structural assessment via drone before sending rescue teams above floor 5
2. Stage medical units at the riverside park, two blocks north
3. Establish a hot zone perimeter; coordinate with traffic control
