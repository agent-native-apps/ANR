# Incident inc-002 — North Warehouse fire and possible chemical release

**Reported by:** First-arriving Engine 22 at 06:51
**Status:** active, escalating
**Casualty estimate:** unknown; warehouse believed unoccupied at time of quake

Two-story industrial warehouse, approximately 80m × 40m, fully involved.
Visible smoke is yellow-tinged. Responders downwind report a pungent,
chlorine-like odor at distances exceeding 200m.

The warehouse is registered as storing industrial cleaning agents and
pool maintenance chemicals. Manifests are not yet available; the
occupant has not been reachable by phone.

A residential block (Riverside Tower, see inc-001) is approximately 300m
northeast and currently being evaluated for medical evacuation due to
the parallel structural emergency.

Recommended actions for command:
1. Treat as potential hazmat incident pending positive identification
2. Hold evacuation routing for inc-001 until prevailing wind direction is confirmed
3. Engage hazmat capability immediately — internal hazmat team capacity is limited
