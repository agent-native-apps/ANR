# Incident inc-003 — Metro Overpass concrete spalling

**Reported by:** Transit Authority dispatch at 07:02
**Status:** active, no injuries
**Casualty estimate:** none confirmed

Inspection by transit personnel after the quake found visible concrete
spalling along the south face of the Metro Overpass between supports 14
and 17. Exposed reinforcing bar is visible at two locations. A piece of
debris approximately 30cm across landed on the roadway below; no
vehicles were struck.

Commuter rail service across the overpass has been suspended pending
structural assessment. Road traffic underneath has been diverted by
local police; one lane remains open with a flag crew.

Recommended actions for command:
1. Drone-based structural assessment of all four supports
2. Coordinate with Transit Authority on service restoration timeline
3. Low priority for life-safety dispatch unless assessment escalates
